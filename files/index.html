Off Script?

Off Script visualizes the linguistic diversity of text by measuring patterns, variance, and complexity through an interactive 3D terrain.

AI writing creates flat valleys.
Human writing creates mountain peaks.

What it analyzes

  • Lexical diversity : how varied your vocabulary is
  • Sentence variance : structural variety in writing
  • Linguistic complexity : unique word patterns
  • AI-like patterns : repetitive or formulaic phrases

This is not an AI detector. It's a visualization tool that shows how diverse or repetitive text patterns are, rendering linguistic "flatness" as a 3D landscape.

Understanding the terrain

Your text becomes a landscape where diverse, complex language creates peaks and repetitive, predictable patterns create valleys.

Color guide:

  • Blue : Very diverse (tall peaks)
  • Green : Diverse
  • Yellow : Neutral
  • Orange : Flat
  • Red : Very flat (deep valleys)

Off Script was created in 2026 to visualize the linguistic patterns that emerge from AI-generated versus human writing.
